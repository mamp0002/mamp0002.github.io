Personal SWOT analysis

By making a Swot-analysis, you analyse your personal strengths and weaknesses and make an inventory of the opportunities and threats you expect in your studies. Below you will see my personal SWOT-analysis.

Strengths: Perseverance, Collaborate, Planning, Structure

Weaknesses: I experience stress fast, everything has to be perfect.

Opportunities: If I can tone down the perfectionism a bit, then it will mean that I can deliver good reliable products because of it.

Threats: That because of the stress, I can't deliver good products. But I expect that I can control that by planning everything well and having a good structure.
