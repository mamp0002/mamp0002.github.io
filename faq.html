Below you will see a list of the most frequently asked questions. When you have a question that is not listed, please contact the helpdesk or look around in the SelfServicePortal.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

By connecting to one of the printers at school.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Use the same printer, scan your paper and it should be added on your pc.

What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

Contact the teachers/helpdesk at school and stay home. Follow the lessons on stream to compensate for that unusual sickness.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book a room on the HZ page after logging in by pressing the Selfservice Portal. Then click "Make a reservation" and there you can, amongst other things, book a project room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You must park your car "across the road", at the parking lot of the former PEZM.
