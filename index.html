Welcome to this website, feel free to look around. If you’d like to get to know more about who I am and what things I like to do, go here. If you scroll down, you’ll see an introduction to my motivation for the choice for ICT. I elaborate more on the topic in some of my blogposts.

Why ICT Field

I tried a studies called "Toegepaste Wiskunde" (Applied Mathematics) a few years ago, but it didn’t work out. In the study programme were some courses related to ICT, and that really grabbed my attention. I have worked a year at Roompot at the Systemmanagement and Project department, where we had to use and maintain (from the front-end) some systems, including:

  • Maxxton Software
  • Newyse
  • Untill
  • ...
I really enjoyed when we had workshops from the different companies where they showed us a quick overview of how they made the systems and changed things. I really liked that part more than only working from the front-end. This is one of the reasons that the ICT field interested me. For the reason why it is the best choice for me, take a look at my Blog-page.

Why an ICT studies

SO I figured out that I wanted to do something with ICT. I tried a couple online courses of Programming, including:

  • Python
  • Visual Basic
  • CSS
  • HTML
  • JavaScript
  • Java
They were really interesting, but when I started the JavaScript and Java courses, things became a lot harder. I decided that it was better to have more support from real-life teachers that I could bother with my questions. That's why I chose for a full-time ICT-programme.
